A lot of people are scared of bats, so given our proximity to Halloween, we thought we would remind you of how cute they are!

Reasons why you should love bats:

  • They can be super cute
  • They are the only mammals that can fly
  • They help produce some of your favourite foods since they are pollinators.
  • They can see with their ears
